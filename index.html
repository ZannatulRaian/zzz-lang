I looked at modern programming languages and thought—that’s a lot of typing. So I built one where the keywords sound like someone half-asleep on a couch. Because that’s who I’m building for.

Why bother

Designed for maximum
minimum effort

yawn to output

Printing shouldn’t require eleven characters and a pair of parentheses. One word. Four letters. Done. Go back to sleep.

if u care

Most conditions don’t matter. But when they do, at least the syntax doesn’t judge you for having standards.

while still awake

Loops that keep going until you’re done. Like your 3am coding session—but it actually has an end.

no types, no semicolons, no rules

zzz doesn’t care about your types. It doesn’t care about your semicolons. It’s too tired to enforce anything.
